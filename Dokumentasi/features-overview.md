# Features Overview (Bab 1 - 31)

Simaju Core mengimplementasikan seluruh blueprint fitur yang ditetapkan dalam `fitur.md`.

## 📋 Ringkasan Bab

### 🧠 Core (Bab 1, 2, 11, 12, 31)
- **Auth System**: Login, Register, Logout, JWT.
- **User Profile**: Identitas, KTP, dan upload dokumen.
- **RBAC**: Role & Permission Middleware.
- **Standard**: Alur Input -> Proses -> Output.

### 🏢 Business (Bab 3, 4, 13, 14, 15)
- **Master Data**: Produk, Kategori, Supplier.
- **Transaction**: Penjualan & Pembelian dengan stok otomatis.
- **Multi-Tenant**: Struktur SaaS ready.
- **Inventory**: Stock movement tracking.

### ⚙️ System (Bab 5, 7, 8, 16, 19, 21)
- **Global Config**: Pengaturan aplikasi & pajak.
- **Logging**: Activity log & Audit trail mendalam.
- **Automation**: Background scheduler (Cron).
- **API Management**: X-API-KEY Auth.

### 🌍 Advanced (Bab 9, 10, 22, 23, 25, 28, 29, 30)
- **SEO**: Meta generator & URL slug.
- **Extension**: Global Event Bus.
- **AI**: Smart suggestion placeholder.
- **i18n**: Sistem multi-bahasa.
- **Custom Field**: Field dinamis per modul.
- **Workflow**: Approval system berjenjang.
- **CMS**: Management artikel & blog.
